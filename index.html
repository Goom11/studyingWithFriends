Studying with Friends

By Aman Agarwal

I was inspired by the support I got from my good friend Kelly Ou and the "Best Friends" sticker pack on Facebook to make a story about studying with friends. Here it is.

Why I Made This

To Remind People

That studying with a buddy makes studying much less scary and that having support is very important when it comes to mental health. I realize that this story to an extent trivializes the anxieties and fears associated with deadlines, grades, exams, and the pursuit of employment. Due to the limited nature of the medium, I was unable to explore and elaborate on these but the purpose of this webpage is not to do that but to simply provide a small reminder that there are those who can support us.
